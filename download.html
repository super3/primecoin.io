Gemmer
Gemmer is a minimalist easy to use multi-wallet supporting Primecoin, Peercoin, V Systems, Bitcoin and many more. Gemmer provides long term support and free listing for blockchain communities.
Google Play Download from Github
Gemmer currently supports Android 64-bit operating system, with community support on Discord.
